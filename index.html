Positioning Statement
We are not only a ticketing vendor. We are the platform partner that enables Premier Parks to run a connected ecosystem. Our responsibility is to own the entitlement backbone end-to-end and create clean interfaces that allow PMS, F&B, identity, and financial systems to plug in without compromising control.
Executive Summary Why Us Why Now Why This Plan
Board-Level Case We own the hardest and most operationally critical layer: ticketing, entitlement logic, and access validation across all channels. The timeline leaves no room for architectural drift; ownership must be explicit before build scale begins. The model is domain-specialized but control-centered: ticketing remains the entitlement backbone while partners integrate through clean contracts.

Business Outcomes We Commit To
What this program must deliver
PRIMARY OUTCOMES
  • One consistent guest journey from online booking to on-property spend and post-visit engagement
  • Unified entitlement rules across passes, dated tickets, add-ons, and cross-park benefits
  • Reliable integrations into Opera PMS, Inntopia, Booker, Anemone, and F&B systems

What Fails Without a Ticketing Core
Key risk statement for the RFP
PROGRAM RISKS
  • No canonical entitlement source creates disputes at gates, POS, and partner systems
  • Inconsistent pass benefit enforcement causes revenue leakage and guest dissatisfaction
  • Disconnected systems multiply reconciliation, exception handling, and manual support
  • Financial cutover risk increases when operational events are not normalized at source

Inntopia
Unified Booking Engine / Commerce Orchestration
New Partner

The master booking orchestration hub. Guests build packages — lodging, tickets, spa, campground — in a single cart. Inntopia queries each system and issues confirmed reservations after payment.

STRATEGIC FIT
  • Only platform purpose-built for multi-product resort packages at scale
  • Direct match for Valcartier, Nashville Shores, and Elitch Gardens where lodging + activities bundling is core
  • Existing Opera PMS relationships reduce integration risk on the accommodation side
  • Keeps package logic out of the ticketing and PMS layers
OPEN QUESTIONS
  • Real-time vs. batch ticket inventory sync with RocketRez — the critical path item
  • Quebec QST + GST stacking and French-language readiness for Canadian properties
  • White-label / per-property branding capability

Spotlio
Dynamic Pricing / Mobile App / Guest Engagement
Existing Partner

An orchestration and guest engagement layer with native dynamic pricing, CRM, and mobile app. Known integration history with RocketRez reduces the cold-start risk you get with a new partner.

STRATEGIC FIT
  • Known integration history with RocketRez — established relationship, not a greenfield build
  • Dynamic pricing and CRM included natively; Inntopia requires a third tool for both
  • Mobile app capability consolidates guest engagement into a single vendor
  • Active references at Morey's Piers, Gausta (Ski Big 3), and Powder Mountain
