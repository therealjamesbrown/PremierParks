Executive Positioning and Delivery Plan

More than ticketing: your strategic transformation partner for Premier Parks

We bring combined decades of attractions experience and will partner with Premier Parks to deliver its vision: move from non-standard, independently managed operations to a repeatable, unified portfolio operating model. Ticketing and entitlements remain the control plane, but our role is broader: design, orchestrate, and execute the full ecosystem across guest experience, operations, and financial governance.

Strategic Partnership

A combined team with deep attractions experience focused on unified guest experience, entitlement control, and portfolio standardization.

The Vision

Move from non-standard, independently managed businesses to a repeatable, unified attractions portfolio model.

Our Role

We are not only a ticketing vendor. We are the transformation partner that owns entitlement control while orchestrating integration across PMS, F&B, identity, and finance.

Preferred Path

Options 1 & 2: Spotlio and Vantage are the leading paths — stronger digital experience, mobile-native capability, and better long-term fit for Premier Parks' guest-facing ambitions. Inntopia remains viable for resort-heavy properties but is not the preferred portfolio-wide choice.

Positioning Statement
We are not only a ticketing vendor. We are the platform partner that enables Premier Parks to run a connected ecosystem. Our responsibility is to own the entitlement backbone end-to-end and create clean interfaces that allow PMS, F&B, identity, and financial systems to plug in without compromising control.
Executive Summary Why Us Why Now Why This Plan
Board-Level Case We own the hardest and most operationally critical layer: ticketing, entitlement logic, and access validation across all channels. The timeline leaves no room for architectural drift; ownership must be explicit before build scale begins. Spotlio and Vantage are the leading paths for Premier Parks — both deliver stronger digital guest experiences, mobile-native capability, and better long-term strategic fit than a pure Inntopia approach.
